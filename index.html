MyBit Go App

With Go we wanted to re-imagine how people generate income.

You live in a place where the electricity doesn't cost that much. So you're thinking maybe I can try running a BTC miner? It costs too much and at the moment you can't buy it by yourself, but you have the time and place and willingness. So you go to Go and list your asset. Select the BTC miner model you want, describe potential ROI and risks, add some collateral. After listing the asset anybody can chip in getting ownership of your BTC miner. After successfully funding you run your miner and share the profits with your peers.

Aggregion

FIRST DESIGNER // AUG 2015 – MAY 2017

Content Owners have a lot of stuff: books, videos, audios, software & access to their secret services. So they want these things to be easy and quick to access by users in exchange of user's money. Definitely Content Owners are afraid that users will share content with each other without permission. Or even pirates will steal their content. So here we come: we are Aggregion and we protect Content Owner's stuff and help to distribute it to end users. Oh, and also there are Resellers, who want to sell various Content Owners' content to broader audience. And of course there are libraries and universities too, who are willing to share their books, videos etc, but need it to be returnable.

Aggregion is quite good at it's job so our clients and partners included Microsoft, Samsung, Intel, Disney and many more small & big publishers and content owners.
